Lifelong Learning Club

About Us

The LifeLong Learning Club is dedicated to providing academic support, professional development, and social activities for NTU EEE MSc students.

Our team members come from diverse backgrounds, contributing to the club's growth and the development of its members.

Events

Lab Tours (ALTi)

We organize tours of NTU and other corporate labs to give students firsthand experience with cutting-edge technology.

Student Support and Engagement Projects

We offer various support projects like the Fruit Sharing Initiative and CAPS airport shuttle service to help MSc students better adapt to their studies and life.

Workshops and Seminars

We hold workshops and seminars to enhance students' professional skills and industry knowledge.

Leadership and Skill Development

Through club activities, we cultivate students' leadership and teamwork abilities.
